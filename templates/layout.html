About

Lyrics2Chords incorporates data from Spotify, HookTheory, APISeeds, and last.fm. Using an AI platform we suggest musical chord progressions.

Input your lyrics to a song you are writing and press submit and you will recieve suggested progressions that will compliment the emotional sentiments of your song. Enjoy!
